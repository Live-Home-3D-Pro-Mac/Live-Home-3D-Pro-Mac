## Overview

Live Home 3D Pro Professional Suite is a complete residential design and visualization platform that serves homeowners and design professionals with the accuracy, rendering quality, and presentation tools needed for serious home planning and design work.

Walkthrough animation creates video tours of the designed space that can be shared with clients or family members who are not able to navigate the 3D environment interactively, communicating the design vision in an accessible format.

Sun position simulation shows how natural light enters and moves through the designed space at any time of day and any season, enabling lighting design decisions informed by real-world solar behavior rather than estimation.

Dimensioned drawing export produces professional floor plans suitable for contractor communication, permit applications, and formal design documentation from the same model used for 3D visualization.

Live Home 3D Pro Professional Suite is the home design platform for users who want the combination of precision drafting, realistic 3D visualization, and professional presentation output in a tool that is approachable for non-architects while being capable enough for professional design work.

## Performance & Integration

Live Home 3D Pro is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Live Home 3D Pro processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
